About Us

Our project, in partnership with ESPRIT, involves analyzing the data of Amazon India, one of the leading players in the e-commerce industry. As data analysts, our task is to help amazon india to:

  • Collect and analyze large volumes of data to identify patterns and trends
  • Improve its marketing strategies
  • Increase sales

Amalyze is a cutting-edge dashboard solution designed specifically for Amazon India. Our platform is designed to provide sellers on Amazon India with powerful tools and analytics to help them grow their businesses and achieve their goals.

functionalities

Our application create user-friendly dashboards to assist with decision-making that will increase profits by minimizing the number of canceled orders and retaining their customers. Our application's features include sales analysis, promotions analysis, delivery analysis, and customer behavior analysis..

Sales informations

You will see an overview of Amazon India sales including revenue, total sales, average basket with the ability to easily filter the data by category, month and year.

Discount informations

Discount information dashboard can help Amazon India to stay competitive, analyze customer behavior, manage inventory, and increase sales and revenue.

Customer satisfaction

How amazon is going to maintain its leading position in the market, it had to continue to satisfy its customers and meet their expectations.

Deliveries informations

Delivery information dashboard can help Amazon India to optimize its delivery process, reduce delivery costs, improve customer satisfaction, and manage its inventory efficiently.
